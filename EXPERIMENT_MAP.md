# Experiment map

## Coordination experiments

### Pistacchio
Social identity / shared commonality intervention before restaurant-choice coordination.
- `pistacchio_same_survey.py`
- `pistacchio_different_survey.py`

### Fast Friends
Coordination experiment that uses the 36 Questions for Increasing Closeness protocol from GGIA / Berkeley instead of generic small talk.
- `fast_friends_same.py`
- `fast_friends_diff.py`

## Trust experiments

### Investment games
Trust-focused interactions with pre/post affective survey measurement. These are about whether shared-identity discussion changes trust, not restaurant coordination.
- `investment_same_survey.py`
- `investment_diff_survey.py`
- `investment_diff_friends.py`
